I started writing these notes because a student wanted a copy what I was using to teach. They didn't know I was working off a sticky note that consisted of about six words and a couple example problems. To avoid that embarrassment, I started putting work into a written form of my lessons, which turned out to be really useful. It forced me to rehearse and prepare, rather than just convince myself I knew a topic and than ramble for 10 minutes without any clear sense of direction.

So, the notes you'll find here are all my thoughts about a topic and answers to questions I have when I read it.
