> ### Basic Rules of probability
>
> The multiplication rule for AND events.
>
> $$\begin{align}
> P(A \text{ and } B) &= P(A|B)\cdot P(B) \\
> \end{align}$$
>
> The addition rule for OR events.
>
> $$\begin{align}
> P(A \text{ or } B)  &= P(A) + P(B) - P(A \text{ and } B)
> \end{align}$$
